Distinguished Bartenders

What does it take to be a great bartender? A talent behind the stick, a creative flair, and a willingness to experiment all help. The following 8 famous bartenders, beyond making exceptional drinks, have gone one step further and changed cocktail culture for the better, whether by writing essential books (like Harry Craddock), or reviving speakeasy culture (like Sasha Petraske). Once you've read up on their accomplishments, do make yourself a tipple to toast with.
