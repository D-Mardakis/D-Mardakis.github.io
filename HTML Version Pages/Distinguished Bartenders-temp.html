Famous Cocktails

What does it take to be a great bartender? A talent behind the stick, a creative flair, and a willingness to experiment all help. But beyond making exceptional drinks, the following 10 famous bartenders have gone one step further and changed cocktail culture for the better, whether by writing essential books (like Harry Craddock), reviving speakeasy culture (like Sasha Petraske), or bringing green innovations to mixology in the modern-day (like Ryan Chetiyawardana). Once you've read up on their accomplishments, do make yourself a tipple to toast with.
